Among Us

The topic of cooperative games in which you need to find a traitor in your ranks is gaining popularity. Although Among Us unblocked games is not a new project, the game developed for a very long time and finally reached the final stage. The game is designed for 4-10 people who, traveling on a spaceship, will find out which of their teams is a traitor. If you have the role of peaceful, then by all means you need to find traitors and destroy them. At the same time, acting aggressively, you will not arouse the suspicions of other civilians who may remove you as a traitor. If you play for a traitor, then you need not to impersonate yourself and at the same time destroy the entire team!
